About

311

311 (pronounced "three eleven") is an American platinum selling rock band from Omaha, Nebraska. With thirteen studio albums, two live albums, four compilation albums, four EPs and four DVDs. under their belts it is no wonder that 311 is one of the most well known Rock bands in the world.


This Fall, 311 will return to the road to play 27 shows in 20 U.S Stages from 9/2 – 11/12 and in 2023 they will set sail on their 7th 311 Caribbean Cruise. For additional information on 311, please visit their website below.
