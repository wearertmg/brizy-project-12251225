About

Cypress Hill

Cypress Hill (founded 1988) is a three time Grammy nominated Latino American hip-hop group based in South Gate, California.


Considered to be one of the forefathers of West Coast rap and 1990s hip hop, this group has released a total of nine critically acclaimed LPs, sold over 20 million albums worldwide, collected an arsenal of multi-platinum and platinum albums.


In 2019 Cypress Hill received the very first star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame for a hip hop group. Their hits include Insane in the Brain, Rock Superstar, How I Could Just Kill A Man, and Real Estate. For additional information on Cypress Hill, please visit their website.
